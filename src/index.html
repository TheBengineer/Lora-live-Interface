What is the Georgia Mesh?

The Georgia Mesh is a low-power, low-cost, open-source mesh network that connects Georgia's 100+ cities. It's designed to be a low-cost, low-power alternative to traditional cellular networks.

The Georgia Mesh is a combination of two protocols: Meshtastic and Meshcore. Both protocols are open-source and free to use.
